Partnership Brief

The 12/17/25 Standard (US 19/424,106)

A documented carbon-negative process utilizing agricultural waste to replace virgin timber in commercial packaging and paper manufacturing.

Priority filing established December 17, 2025. Addresses California SB 54 (Extended Producer Responsibility), SB 253 (Climate Corporate Data Accountability), and SB 261 (Climate-Related Financial Risk) mandates effective January 1, 2026.

Licensed entities can align with current reporting mandates while participating in the Arboreum Impact Foundation (AIF) 85% profit distribution model.
