Partnership Brief

The 12/17 Standard

A verified carbon-negative process utilizing agricultural waste to replace virgin timber in commercial packaging and paper manufacturing.

Priority filing established December 17, 2025. Addresses California SB 54 (Extended Producer Responsibility), SB 253 (Climate Corporate Data Accountability), and SB 261 (Climate-Related Financial Risk) mandates effective January 1, 2026.

Licensed entities achieve immediate compliance while participating in the Arboreum Impact Fund (AIF) 85% profit distribution model.

August 10, 2026 Deadline: 15/15 licensing terms available for early partnership commitments. Post-deadline terms subject to standard commercial rates.
For Operations Leadership

  • Drop-in replacement for virgin timber feedstock
  • Verified carbon-negative classification under CA regulatory framework
  • 85% revenue participation through AIF structure
  • Immediate SB 54/SB 253/SB 261 compliance certification
