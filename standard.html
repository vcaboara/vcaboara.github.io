The 12/17 Standard

Industrial Protocol for Virgin Timber Displacement (VTD)

Executive Summary: The 12/17 Standard provides a verified, carbon-negative path for the global packaging industry to transition from high-risk virgin timber to agricultural waste feedstocks. It addresses the immediate "Compliance Cliff" created by CA SB 54, EU PPWR, and emerging PFAS litigation.

I. Global Compliance Matrix

North America: SB 54 (CA), SB 253 (CA), EPA PFAS Reporting (Federal).

Europe: EU PPWR (Recyclability Grade A), EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR).

Industrial: Decarbonization of "Hard-to-Abate" sectors via 12/17 Thermal Recovery.
