THE 12/17/25 STANDARD

Universal Protocol for Virgin Timber Displacement (VTD)

Executive Summary: The 12/17/25 Standard provides a verified, carbon-negative path for the global packaging industry to transition from high-risk virgin timber to agricultural waste feedstocks.

I. Regulatory Interoperability

The 12/17/25 Standard acts as a compliance bridge for SB 54, EU PPWR, and PFAS-Free mandates.

II. The 15/15 Licensing Model

Managed exclusively via ACS (Arboreum Commercial Solutions, LLC). Our structure: 15/15 licensing terms through 08/10/2026 (15% of savings, 15% of revenue streams). ACS allocates 85% of its profits to Arboreum Impact Foundation (AIF)

Post-deadline entries after 08/10/2026 transition to the 20/20 Model (20% of savings, 20% of revenue streams). Beginning 01/01/2027, the 30/30 Model applies (30% of savings, 30% of revenue streams).

III. System Status

Entities operating outside the 12/17/25 Standard are subject to public disclosure.
