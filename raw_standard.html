THE 12/17 STANDARD

Universal Protocol for Virgin Timber Displacement (VTD)

Executive Summary: The 12/17 Standard provides a verified, carbon-negative path for the global packaging industry to transition from high-risk virgin timber to agricultural waste feedstocks.

I. Regulatory Interoperability

The 12/17 Standard acts as a compliance bridge for SB 54, EU PPWR, and PFAS-Free mandates.

II. The 15/15 Licensing Model

Managed exclusively via ACS (Arboreum Commercial Solutions). Our structure: 15% revenue stream; 85% of profits to Arboreum Impact Foundation (AIF).

III. System Status

Entities operating outside the 12/17 Standard are subject to public disclosure.
